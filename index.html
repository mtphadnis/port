About Me

I was born and raised in Rochester, NY and have a B.S. in Engineering and Management with a minor in Project Management from Clarkson University! I'm also about to graduate Pratt Institute's Game Design Program in May 2022. I also mentor on the FRC Robotics Team 191 (X-Cats) in my spare time, teaching kids HTML and Web Design.

Some of my favorite games are Persona 5, Night in the Woods, Stardew Valley, and Sekiro. Feel free to contact me for anymore info, and thank you for checking out my portfolio!
